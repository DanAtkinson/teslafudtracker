FUD is an acronym for fear, uncertainty and doubt. It's a disinformation marketing strategy used to cast a shadow over a competitor or entity threatening your own success. If you have shorted, compete against or will be impacted by Tesla it makes sense to try and destroy their image and slow them down as much as possible.

There are plenty of media outlets happy to lend themselves as FUD factories and have ramped up their own production. The purpose of this tracker is to keep score on which sources and authors publish FUD that can be ignored and which stay within the bounds of objective journalism.

The tracker works by matching common FUD patterns in articles, it does not look for click-bait or bad news.
